Overview of Information Systems Programs

Bachelor of Science in Information Systems (BS-IS)

The Bachelor of Science in Information Systems (BS-IS) at Cleveland State University is a comprehensive, four-year STEM-designated undergraduate program designed for those who want to leverage technology to drive organizational success. This program offers a unique blend of technology and business principles, equipping students with the skills needed to succeed in today‚Äôs rapidly evolving digital landscape.

Career Paths: Business Analyst, IT Consultant, Systems Developer, Technical Support Specialist

Sample Job Roles: Help Desk Analyst, Data Analyst, Applications Support Specialist

Master of Science in Information Systems (MIS)

A specialized STEM program, Cleveland State University's Master of Information Systems (MIS) degree prepares students to advance their careers as information professionals. Program concentrations allow students to explore their interests and build specific skill sets in areas such as information security, information technology management and business analytics.

Career Paths: IT Manager, Data Architect, Enterprise Systems Analyst, Project Manager

Sample Job Roles: Data Scientist, Systems Analyst, Chief Information Officer
