Apply to the Information Systems Department

Undergraduate Admissions

Eligibility Requirements: Applicants must have a high school diploma or equivalent with a competitive GPA, and meet CSU's general admission standards.

Application Deadlines:

  • Fall 2025 Semester: July 1, 2025
  • Spring 2026 Semester: December 2, 2025
  • Summer 2025 Semester: April 13, 2025

Apply Online: CSU Undergraduate Application Portal

Graduate Admissions

GRE/GMAT Requirements: GRE/GMAT scores may be required based on GPA and prior coursework. Please check site for latest requirements.

Prerequisites: A bachelor's degree in IS or a related field. Students without a relevant background may be required to complete foundation courses.

Scholarships & Financial Aid

IS Student Scholarships: CSU offers department scholarships for qualified Information Systems students based on academics and financial need.

External Opportunities: We encourage students to explore scholarships from organizations such as ISACA Foundation and local business partners. Check scholarship information for more details.
